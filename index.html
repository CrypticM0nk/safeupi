About Us

Fraud awareness starts with your messages

Digital payment scams in India often begin with a simple SMS — a fake bank alert, a phishing link disguised as a KYC reminder, or a message impersonating a known sender. By the time most people realise something is wrong, the damage is done.

SafeUPI was built to change that. Our platform monitors transaction-related SMS alerts received on your device and analyses them for known fraud indicators — suspicious links, spoofed sender IDs, fake urgency, and more. When a threat is detected, you get an immediate warning.

We don't interfere with your payments or access your bank. We simply watch your messages, and tell you when something looks wrong.

How It Works

From SMS to safety in seconds

SafeUPI works quietly in the background — watching your inbox so you don't have to.

1

A payment SMS arrives on your phone

Every UPI transaction, bank debit, or payment-related alert generates an SMS on your device. SafeUPI monitors these messages as they arrive — nothing is uploaded or shared externally.

AD-HDFCBK
Your a/c XX4821 debited ₹4,500 on 14-Jun for UPI ref 3291847563. Avl bal ₹18,200.
2

SafeUPI analyses it for fraud indicators

Our system scans the message text against a continuously updated library of scam patterns — suspicious links, spoofed sender IDs, fake urgency phrases, impersonation keywords, and unusual UPI address formats.

3

Suspicious patterns are flagged

If the message contains indicators of fraud, each one is identified and tagged — so you know exactly what looks wrong and why.

UNKNOWN
URGENT: Your UPI ID will be suspended in 2 hours. Click http://upi-secure.xyz to verify KYC or lose access.

4

You receive an instant warning notification

SafeUPI pushes an alert to your device immediately — before you have a chance to click a link, share an OTP, or make a payment. The notification explains what was found and what to avoid.
